The Goldilocks Era –
Balancing Growth,
Complexity, and Opportunity
Private credit is entering a transformative phase, with the global market
expected to surpass $1.6 trillion in assets under management in 2025. This
growth is driven by its expanding role in M&A activity, where larger lenders are
facilitating increasingly complex transactions. As private credit penetrates
new asset classes and refines innovative deal structures, it offers investors
enhanced opportunities to achieve stable, risk - adjusted returns.
Regulatory shifts are also reshaping the landscape, promoting greater
transparency and influencing market practices. Institutional interest in
private credit is surging, with allocations projected to rise significantly as
allocators seek diversification an d protection against macroeconomic
uncertainty.

11 : 20 AM – 12 : 0 0 PM

Private Credit at a Crossroads: Adaptation, Innovation & Investor Strategy
Lakeview Junior Ballroom
As private credit approaches a $2 trillion global market, institutional investors are adjusting to a
rapidly evolving landscape. This panel will examine how investors are refining their strategies in
response to key developments shaping the sector in 2025. Discussion will cover the growing
convergence between public and private markets and its impact on portfolio diversification, the
rise of specialty finance and niche credit strategies as avenues for higher yields and diversification,
and the challenges po sed by increasing regulatory oversight . Panelists will share insights on
managing risks and capitalizing on emerging opportunities in the private credit space.
