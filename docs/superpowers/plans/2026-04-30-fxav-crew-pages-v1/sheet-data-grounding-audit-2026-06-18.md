# Audit: Spec/Plan Data Assumptions NOT Grounded in the Live Test Sheets

**Scope:** 188 extracted assumptions verified against 7 real Doug-template "II"/"AII/III" shows pulled live via the gsheets MCP.
**Verdict totals:** 71 grounded · 22 contradicted · 70 partial · 23 unverifiable (117 non-grounded findings detailed below).

## 1. Executive summary

The spec/plan are broadly correct about *what fields exist* but systematically wrong about *uniformity and era boundaries*. Three failure classes dominate the non-grounded verdicts:

### 1a. AGENDA "empty scaffolding" is FALSE for 2 of 7 shows (the headline tension)
The pervasive project assumption — encoded in `AGENDA-7`, `ASSUMP_SCHEMA-13/14`, and the IA redesign — that the AGENDA tab is always empty scaffolding is **contradicted live**. What the AGENDA tabs actually contain across the corpus:

| Show | AGENDA fill state |
|---|---|
| **East Coast** (2024) | **DENSE** — full sessions: `AGENDA!G6` = `8:15 AM / 8:30 AM / 0:15 / "Welcome and Introductory Remarks" / Mabel 1 / POD`; `G7` = `"Opening Keynote" / Mabel 1 / LAV`; populated DAY-2 block (`M5:R11`) incl `"To Own a Professional Sport Team:"`. ~23 populated body rows. |
| **RIA Central** (2025-06) | **DENSE** — `AGENDA!G6` = `"Attendee Registration and Breakfast"`; `"Are Trump 2.0, Jay Powell and the AI Revolution…"` / Salon / 5 Goosenecks; ~18 session rows. |
| **Redefining FI** (2025-05) | Empty skeleton — START/FINISH/TRT only; all TITLE/ROOM/AV blank. |
| **Consultants** (2025-10) | Empty skeleton; `A3`/`A4` carry `#REF!` errors. |
| **Fixed Income** (2025-10) | Empty skeleton. |
| **FinTech** (2026) | Empty skeleton. |
| **RPAS** (2026) | Empty skeleton. |

So empty-scaffolding is the *majority* case (5/7) but NOT universal. Crucially, the AGENDA-tab grid is **distinct from** the dense inline hourly agenda the 2026 shows pack into the INFO `TIME / AGENDA` col E (`ASSUMP_SCHEMA-13`, grounded: RPAS `INFO!E21` ~16 timestamps, FinTech `INFO!E22` ~11). Two different surfaces both carry schedule data on different shows.

The crew-facing agenda surface remains the **linked PDF** (`AGENDA-5`, grounded) — every show references one via the INFO AGENDA-LINK row — so the IA "don't wire the AGENDA tab" decision can stand as a product choice, but its *justifying premise* ("empty scaffolding") is factually wrong for East Coast + RIA.

### 1b. "2026-only" / date-based version markers are really TEMPLATE-version markers (the Fixed Income trap)
Multiple era claims cite a calendar-year boundary that the **2025-10 Fixed Income Trading Summit** breaks — it already uses the "v4" compact template:
- `ASSUMP_SCHEMA-06` (contradicted): "Contact Office + MAIN/SECONDARY are 2026 only" — Fixed Income (2025-10) has `INFO!A10='Contact Office'`, `B7='MAIN'`/`C7='SECONDARY'`, `D6='ENTER DATES BELOW'`, and even `D12='SPREADSHEET FROM LAST YEAR'`.
- `ASSUMP_SCHEMA-22` (contradicted): structured HOTEL RESERVATION block is a template, not a 2026 marker — Fixed Income `INFO!A41='HOTEL'` with RESERVATION #1/#2.
- `ASSUMP_SCHEMA-26` (contradicted): 8-stage transport grid is template-not-year — Fixed Income has all 8 stages (Rental Pickup…Rental Return).
- `ASSUMP_SCHEMA-23` (contradicted): MAIN/SECONDARY appears in 2025-10, not just 2026.
- Any year-based version inference will misclassify Fixed Income. Branch on **structure presence** (DATE/TIME sub-header, Contact Office cell), never on the date.

### 1c. The crew-email identity model has source data in only 1 of 7 shows (P0 for auth)
`SP-029` / `ASSUMP_SCHEMA-08` (contradicted) + `AP-02`/`AP-04`/`AP-17`/`AP-18` (high-severity partial/contradicted): crew email is **NOT** required from 2025-06+. Only **RIA** populates crew emails (`INFO!E21:E23` = dlarson@fxav.net / edweiss412@gmail.com / calvindsaller@gmail.com). The other 6 shows have null or absent crew email. Eric Weiss is crew on **all 7** shows by name but his email is recorded only on RIA — so a single sign-in matching by email would surface only the RIA show. **OAuth-email identity matching has no source data on 6 of 7 live shows;** identity must fall back to name-selection (consistent with the picker pivot). Phone is similarly absent on 3 of 7.

### 1d. PULL SHEET detection has a CRITICAL self-contradiction
`PC-033` + `SP-034` (critical): RIA Central's tab literally named **`PULL SHEET`** carries header `A7:G7 = [QTY, PULLED, INITAL, CAT, SUB CAT, ITEM, NOTES]`. The parser at `lib/parser/pull-sheet.ts` detects PULL SHEET by "every header cell == PULL SHEET" AND explicitly **excludes** any table containing `PULLED`+`INITAL` (treating it as GEAR). RIA's PULL SHEET contains both tokens → it would be **wrongly excluded**. The `SP-033`/`SCH-13`/`ASSUMP_SCHEMA-19` "PULL SHEET absent from 2025-06+" era claim is also contradicted by RIA (June 2025).

## 2. Corpus & the uniformity problem

The 7 shows do **not** share a fixed tab set, and several specs assume one. Observed tab inventories diverge sharply:

| Show | Era | Tabs (notable) | Crew block | AGENDA | Gear surface |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Redefining FI | 2025-05 | INFO/AGENDA/DIAGRAMS/FORM/**OLD PULL SHEET**/LIST/CONTACTS/DROP DOWN | CREW, no EMAIL col | empty | stale OLD PULL SHEET |
| Consultants | 2025-10 | INFO/GEAR/AGENDA/DIAGRAMS/FORM/LIST/CONTACTS/DROP DOWN | CREW, no EMAIL col | empty (#REF! in A3/A4) | GEAR date-matrix |
| FinTech | 2026 | 14 tabs incl VENUE/CLIENT/TECH (all `#REF!`), VEHICLE/ROLE/TASK | CREW + EMAIL col (blank) + col-F TRUE | empty | GEAR |
| East Coast | 2024-05 | INFO/AGENDA/DIAGRAMS/**PULL SHEET**(~700 rows)/TRAVEL/TO DO | **TECH** (shifted to col B, ARRIVAL/DEPARTURE) | **DENSE** | PULL SHEET (positional, flag-first) |
| RIA Central | 2025-06 | 11 tabs incl **PULL SHEET**, CONTACTS, ITEMS/2nd/3rd Level | CREW + EMAIL col (populated!) | **DENSE** | PULL SHEET (7-col header) |
| Fixed Income | 2025-10 | 12 tabs incl GEAR, VENUE/CLIENT/TECH (`#REF!`), VEHICLE/ROLE | CREW + EMAIL (blank) | empty | GEAR date-matrix |
| RPAS | 2026 | 14 tabs incl GEAR, VENUE/CLIENT/TECH (`#REF!`), VEHICLE/ROLE/TASK | CREW + EMAIL (blank) + col-F TRUE | empty | GEAR date-matrix |

**Implications for any "fixed structure" assumption:**
- **No fixed tab set.** `ASSUMP_SCHEMA-05` (partial): VEHICLE/ROLE are *present* in 2026 (FinTech+RPAS), contradicting "gone in 2026." CONTACTS is present only on the 2025 non-GEAR shows.
- **Backend tabs return `#REF!`** on all three "v4" shows (VENUE 63 cols, CLIENT 86, TECH 38 all yield a single `#REF!` at A1). A position-driven or tab-blind parser must tolerate these.
- **East Coast is the structural outlier:** TECH crew block (not CREW), shifted one column right (data in B-E, A blank), dense AGENDA, no FORM tab, no structured hotel grid, conflated show-day date cell. The 13-fixture corpus likely does not capture this variant (`ASSUMP_SCHEMA-01`, partial).
- **One sheet ≠ one show** universally (`SCH-01`, partial): Redefining FI bundles two co-located programs (RFI + Private Credit Forum) — `INFO!A1` carries both names, `A34`/`A35` reference two separate agenda PDFs.
- **Parser must be section-anchored (label-driven), not position-driven** (`SCH-36`, partial) — broken backend tabs + divergent tab sets + column-shifted crew defeat any positional model.

## 4. Completeness critique — what was NOT verified

This audit read primarily the **INFO crew/details/dates/transport/hotel/venue sections, the AGENDA-tab grid, PULL SHEET/GEAR/DIAGRAMS tabs, and tab metadata** for 7 shows. Gaps a fresh reviewer should close:

- **VENUES master-directory region** (`ASSUMP_SCHEMA-33`) — never targeted; the multi-column reference list (City/State/Catering/Wifi/etc.) is distinct from the per-show INFO venue block and was not located.
- **FORM/LIST/DROP DOWN/ITEMS/2nd-3rd Level/VEHICLE/ROLE/TASK** helper tabs read only at metadata level; their cell content (and whether the parser ever ingests them) is unconfirmed. `SP-010`/`SP-013` hinge on the LIST checklist carrying 'Virtaul Audience' — that single cell was cited from summary, not independently re-read across all LIST-bearing shows.
- **The #REF! backend tabs** (VENUE/CLIENT/TECH on the 3 v4 shows) were confirmed to return `#REF!` at A1 only; whether any *populated* cell exists deeper in them was not probed.

**Unverifiable verdicts that deserve a deeper, non-sheet probe (23 total):**
- **Drive-layer claims** (`SCH-29` image byte-stability, `SP-040/042` image counts, `SCH-28/37` linked folder, `SCH-34` modifiedTime monotonicity, `SCH-35` folder file count) all require `spreadsheets.get` (embeddedObjects) and Drive `files.list/files.get` — the gsheets `sheets_get_values` surface cannot answer them. These are load-bearing for the diagrams/asset feature and the sync watermark and remain genuinely open.
- **Markdown-export pipeline claims** (`PC-035` &#10; entities, `PC-036` separator-row stripping, `PC-003` flat tab-unaware scan, `SCH-36` workbook-identical structure) need verification against `fixtures/shows/raw/*.md` AND the actual exporter, since the live API returns `\n`/`\r\n` not the `&#10;` form the parser expects. **The parser consumes markdown, but this audit read the live API — a fidelity gap that means parser-internal claims (`PC-*`) were verified for *data shape*, not for *what the exporter actually emits*.**
- **All app/schema behavioral claims** (`AP-07/14/15/16/21/22`, `SP-032`, `SCH-30` read-whole, `AGENDA-4` render timing) are code/DB contracts not testable from cells — they need code/test inspection.

- **Rooms attribute completeness** (`AP-09` "7 rooms") was explicitly out of scope on the crew-focused pass; the room-block per-attribute population (audio/video/lighting per GS/breakout) was only spot-checked, not enumerated per show.

**Net:** the parser-structure assumptions (PC-*) are largely sound *for data shape*; the spec's **era/version dichotomies and the email-keyed identity model** are the two areas where live data most sharply contradicts the written assumptions, and the **Drive-layer + markdown-export surfaces** are the largest remaining unverified frontier.
