02 Why this phase is shaped this way
Why a nav registry?

One extra file, but it’s the only way the sidebar, the breadcrumb, and the structural meta-test can agree on what pages exist. Three sources of truth drift the moment anyone adds a page.

Why mirror admin layout verbatim?

The exact requireAdmin() / AdminInfraError shape at app/admin/layout.tsx:47–71 is what tests/auth/_metaInfraContract.test.ts already pins structurally. Mirroring it costs nothing and keeps the new surface inside the existing guarantee.

Why stubs in A.7 (not later)?

The nav-consistency meta-test is the gate; it can only go green if every slug resolves. Phase E replaces stub content with real copy. Defining the stubs at Phase A means Phase E never creates new files — it edits in place.
