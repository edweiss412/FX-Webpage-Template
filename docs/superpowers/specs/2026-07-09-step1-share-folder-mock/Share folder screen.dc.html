Share your show folder

?

The app reads sheets out of one Google Drive folder you pick. Share that folder with the email below so the app can see what is inside.

  1. 1 In Google Drive, find the folder where you keep your show sheets (or make a new one).
    Don’t have a folder yet?
    1. In Google Drive, click New → Folder and give it a name (your show name works well).
    2. Open the folder and drop your show sheet(s) inside.
    3. Share the folder with the email below and give it Viewer access, then come back here.
  2. 2 Click “Share” on the folder.
  3. 3 Paste this email and give it Viewer access:
    fxav-reader@fxav-crew-pages.iam.gserviceaccount.com
    Why this email?

    It is the app’s identity inside your Drive. It can only see the one folder you share with it, and only what is inside it. Removing the share at any time revokes the app’s access.

  4. 4 Come back here and click “I’ve shared the folder.”
