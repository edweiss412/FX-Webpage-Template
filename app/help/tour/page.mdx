Review queues hold the exceptions: first-seen sheets the app held for your sign-off, and staged edits to a sheet that's already live. Each queue is scoped to one show, so working through one feels like triaging a short list rather than scanning the full sheet.</p>

    <h3 className="text-lg font-semibold text-text-strong mb-2">Parse warnings</h3>
    <p className="text-text leading-relaxed mb-3">Parse warnings are softer than queue items. They flag values in a sheet that parsed successfully but look unusual: a missing call time, an unrecognized department, or a date outside the show window. A warning clears on the next sync once you fix the underlying value, or you can leave it if the value is intentional.</p>

The sharing tools let you copy each person's link one row at a time, paste it into whatever channel you already use, and issue a fresh link if someone forwards theirs outside the crew.</p>
