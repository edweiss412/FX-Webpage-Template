# Tour

A one-paragraph orientation to every operator-facing surface in FXAV. Read it once to know what each page is for, then follow the "Read more" link when you want the full reference for a surface.

## Dashboard

The dashboard is the home screen after sign-in. It lists every show currently visible to FXAV, the parse state of each one, and any queues that need your attention. Counts in the header tiles tell you at a glance whether anything is waiting on you.

[Read more →](/help/admin/dashboard)

## Review queues

When the parser cannot confidently assign a crew member to a row, or when an existing crew member needs your sign-off before going live, the row lands in a review queue. Each queue is scoped to one show and one decision class, so working through a queue feels like triaging a short list rather than scanning a sheet.

[Read more →](/help/admin/review-queues)

## Parse warnings

Parse warnings are softer than queue items. They flag rows that parsed successfully but look unusual, such as a missing call time, an unrecognized department, or a date outside the show window. You can clear a warning, edit the underlying row, or ignore it for now.

[Read more →](/help/admin/parse-warnings)

## Per-show panel

The per-show panel is where you spend most of your time once a show is live. It groups every setting and every roster decision for that one show into a single workspace: sync settings, crew list, link policy, and recent activity.

[Read more →](/help/admin/per-show-panel)

## Preview as crew

Preview as crew lets you load any crew member's personal page exactly as they would see it on their phone. It is the fastest way to confirm that a fix you just made shows up correctly, and the safest way to answer "what does Doug see?" without asking Doug.

[Read more →](/help/admin/preview-as-crew)

## Sharing crew links

Each crew member has a personal link that is unique, signed, and revocable. The sharing tools let you copy each person's link one row at a time, paste it into whatever channel you already use to reach that crew member, and issue a fresh link if someone forwards theirs outside the crew.

[Read more →](/help/admin/sharing-links)

## Onboarding wizard

The onboarding wizard walks you through the first-time setup for a new FXAV environment: service-account credentials, the source folder, and the operator allowlist. It is a three-step flow and only runs once per environment.

[Read more →](/help/admin/onboarding-wizard)
