# Sharing crew links

Each show has one share-link for the crew. Send that URL to everyone who needs the show page. When a crew member opens it, the picker asks them to choose their own name, and the page stores that choice for future visits on the same device.

<Callout type="warning">
Use the app's share-link instead of sending the raw Drive sheet URL. Crew see role-filtered show details through the app, not the sheet.
</Callout>

## What the controls do today

The per-show panel's Share & access section carries the current URL and the controls that manage it.

<Step n={1}>
**Current share-link.** This panel shows the URL for the show. Use **Copy** to put it on your clipboard, then send it through your normal crew channel.
</Step>

<Step n={2}>
**Rotate share-token.** Use this when the URL leaks or you want a fresh URL for the show. Rotation makes the old share-link stop working and shows a new one immediately.
</Step>

<Step n={3}>
**Reset picker selections.** Use this when a crew member picked the wrong name or needs to choose again. It does not rotate the show URL.
</Step>

## What crew see

When a crew member opens the share-link, they choose their name from the picker or sign in with Google if their identity has already been claimed. After that, the crew page renders their call time, hotel and room if applicable, schedule, and the contact info they need for their role.

The picker choice is show-specific. If the sheet changes after someone first opens the link, the page re-checks the current roster and show version on later visits.

## Link expiry + revocation

Share-links do not have a fixed calendar expiry. They stay valid while the show is active and while the current share-token remains in place.

Three things can block access:

- **You rotate the share-token.** The previous show URL stops working immediately.
- **You reset picker selections.** Existing picker choices are cleared, so crew members must pick again on their next visit.
- **The crew member is removed from the show's sheet.** The roster change prevents that person from continuing through the picker for that show.

When someone cannot access the page, send the current share-link again or reset picker selections if they picked the wrong name.
