# Per-show panel

`/admin/show/<slug>` is the per-show parse panel. You land here from the dashboard, either by opening a show row or by following a **Review staged changes** badge. The panel is organized as four stacked sections, top to bottom: a staged-review card (only when there are staged edits), sync health, parse warnings, and crew preview links. On a quiet show the staged-review card is absent and the other three sections give you a quick read of what the sheet looks like to the app.

<h2 id="staged-review-card">Staged review card</h2>

The yellow **Action required** card appears at the top of the panel whenever the app has staged edits for this show that haven't been Applied yet. It lists every triggered review item with a plain-language description, shows the diff between the prior approved version and the staged one for each affected section (crew, hotels, rooms, and so on), and gives you the Apply and Discard buttons.

The card also names the version you're approving: "staged from edits Doug made on …" with the staged modified time, so there's no guessing about which save you're looking at. While the card is present, the three sections below render underneath as informational context.

For a full walkthrough of the Apply versus Discard decision, see [Review queues](/help/admin/review-queues#re-stage).

<h2 id="sync-health">Sync health</h2>

A compact list of the last five sync attempts for this show. Each row shows when the sync ran, how long it took, and whether it ended cleanly, with warnings, or with a hard error.

A **Re-sync now** button sits next to the list. Use it when you've just edited the sheet and don't want to wait for the next scheduled poll, or when a previous attempt errored and you've fixed the cell it complained about.

If syncs keep failing in a row, the most likely cause is in the sheet itself: a renamed tab, a column the parser expected that's gone missing, or a row shape change the parser doesn't recognize. Open the sheet in the same tab via the dashboard's row action, fix the cell the error points at, and re-sync. If the failures don't have an obvious cause in the sheet, send the page link and the error text to Eric.

## Parse warnings

A list of warnings the parser flagged on the most recent sync. These are soft signals, not hard failures. The sync still applied and crew can still see the page. For the catalog of warning meanings and what to do about each one, see [Parse warnings](/help/admin/parse-warnings).

## Crew preview links

One link per crew member parsed from the sheet, labeled with the crew member's name and role. Tapping a link opens the show as that crew member would see it, with a sticky **Previewing as …** banner so you never mistake a preview for the real page. Use this to confirm a fix landed for a specific person without sending them anything, or to spot-check role-based filtering before a tour day starts.
