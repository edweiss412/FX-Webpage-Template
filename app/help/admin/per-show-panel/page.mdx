# Per-show panel

`/admin/show/<slug>` is the per-show parse panel. You land here from the dashboard, either by opening a show row or by following a **Review staged changes** badge. The panel is organized as five stacked sections, top to bottom, plus an optional staged-review card that surfaces above them when needed: sync health, preview as a crew member, parse warnings, share and access, and the crew section. On a quiet show the staged-review card is absent and the sections below give you a quick read of what the sheet looks like to the app.

<h2 id="staged-review-card">Staged review card</h2>

A staged-review card appears at the top of the panel whenever the app has staged edits for this show that haven't been Applied yet. It carries an eyebrow telling you where the change came from (**Auto sync**, **Drive push**, **Manual sync**, or **Onboarding scan**), a one-line parse summary as its heading, and lists every triggered review item with a plain-language description. It also shows the diff between the prior approved version and the staged one for each affected section (crew, hotels, rooms, and so on), and gives you the Apply and Discard buttons.

The card also names the version you're approving: "staged from edits Doug made on …" with the staged modified time, so there's no guessing about which save you're looking at. While the card is present, the three sections below render underneath as informational context.

For a full walkthrough of the Apply versus Discard decision, see [Review queues](/help/admin/review-queues#re-stage).

<h2 id="sync-health">Sync health</h2>

A compact list of the last five sync attempts for this show. Each row shows when the sync ran, how long it took, and whether it ended cleanly, with warnings, or with a hard error.

A **Re-sync from Drive** button sits at the top of the page. Tap it when you've just edited the sheet and don't want to wait for the next scheduled poll, or when a previous attempt errored and you've fixed the cell it complained about. While it runs, the label flips to "Syncing…" and a success line ("Synced. Changes applied." or similar, depending on outcome) appears below the button.

If syncs keep failing in a row, the most likely cause is in the sheet itself: a renamed tab, a column the parser expected that's gone missing, or a row shape change the parser doesn't recognize. Open the sheet directly in Google Drive, fix the cell the error points at, and come back here to tap **Re-sync from Drive**. If the failures don't have an obvious cause in the sheet, send the page link and the error text to Eric.

<h2 id="preview-as-crew-member">Preview as a crew member</h2>

A read-only entry point that opens the show through a specific crew member's eyes, exactly as they would see it on their phone. Each crew member parsed from the sheet gets a row with a **Preview as** button; tapping a row drops you onto that person's crew page in impersonation mode with a sticky **Previewing as ...** banner so you never mistake a preview for the real page. For the longer walkthrough see [Preview as crew](/help/admin/preview-as-crew).

## Parse warnings

A list of warnings the parser flagged on the most recent sync. These are soft signals, not hard failures. The sync still applied and crew can still see the page. For the catalog of warning meanings and what to do about each one, see [Parse warnings](/help/admin/parse-warnings).

<h2 id="crew-preview-links">Crew section and share access</h2>

The Share & access section shows the **Current share-link** for the show, a **Copy** button, a **Rotate share-token** control, and **Reset picker selections** for cases where someone picked the wrong identity. Below it, the crew section lists one row per crew member parsed from the sheet, labeled with the crew member's name and role. The preview-as entry point for any specific crew member lives in the [Preview as a crew member](#preview-as-crew-member) section above, not in this section. For the deeper sharing workflow follow [Sharing crew links](/help/admin/sharing-links).
