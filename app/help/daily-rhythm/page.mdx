# Your new daily rhythm

Most days you open the admin dashboard, glance at it for a few seconds, and move on. The app handles the heavy lifting. You handle anything yellow.

## What to glance at

- **Active shows panel.** Each row shows the show name, dates, crew count, and a last-sync status; "Synced" means the last sync succeeded. A yellow **Changes to review** badge means the app held back your latest sheet edits for your review.
- **Needs attention** inbox. Brand-new sheets the app couldn't fully process yet, usually a parse warning or a structural difference the app wants your sign-off on.
- **Changes to review** badge on any active show row. Same idea as the inbox above, but for sheets the app already published once.

## When something is yellow

<Callout type="warning">
  **Yellow means "Doug, take a look."** A yellow **Changes to review** badge means new edits in your
  sheet are waiting for your approval before the app publishes them to crew. Read [Review
  queues](/help/admin/review-queues) for what to look at and when to Apply vs. Discard.
</Callout>

## Sharing a new sheet with crew

Once a show appears in Active shows, each crew member has a personalized page. Copy the current show share-link from the Share & access section on the per-show panel, then send it through your normal crew channel. Details and rules are at [Sharing crew links](/help/admin/sharing-links).

## What hasn't changed

- You still **edit your sheet** the same way. The app reads from it. It does not write back.
- **COI tracking, hotel rosters, transport sheets**, and anything that lives outside the show template stays in whatever tools you already use.
- **Crew communication** outside the page itself, such as texts, calls, and last-minute changes, keeps flowing through your usual channels.
